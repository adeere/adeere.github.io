Census Data

For this project I used census data to look at different factors. I looked at median monthly rent, the percentage of the population taking transit to work, the population, median income, median cost of dwellings, the number of immigrants, and the average age in dissemination areas. I started my project with census tracts but decided to use dissemination areas as they are smaller and show a more diverse population. As census tracts are bigger, some factors often get averaged out to the median and cannot tell (wow this sounds so bad lol). I was aware of the modifiable areal unit problem during this project, as such, the results that I got could have been completely different if I used a different area. I am going to go through each of the census data statistics that I looked at, and explain how it could be related to low elevations.
