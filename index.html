Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to the homepage for my GEOB 370 Final Project. In this project, I looked at how Vancouver will be affected by rising sea levels due to climate change. Much of my analysis is rather unrealistic, and looks at a catastrophic level of sea level rise. I look at different populations based on a number of parameters (age, income, housing price, immigration status...) and look to see if there is a noticeable difference by how more vulnerable people may be affected by climate change.
